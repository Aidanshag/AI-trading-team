# ZW - CBOT Wheat SRW

## Contract specs
- CBOT / ZW; 5000 bu per contract
- Tick: 0.25 cents/bu = $12.50
- Months: Mar, May, Jul, Sep, Dec
- RTH 09:30-13:15 CT, Globex 24h

## What it is
Winter wheat futures (planted Oct-Nov, harvest Jun-Jul). Global commodity tied to Russia/Ukraine supply (25% global production, 35% exports). Unlike corn, wheat is international. Harvested in Jul, Dec is new-crop expectations.

## Primary drivers (2024-2026)
1. Global wheat stocks - Russia/Ukraine production/exports critical
2. US acreage (USDA early Dec) and condition (weekly Jan-Jul)
3. US yield (WASDE monthly, NASS weekly)  
4. Crude (weak, 0.15-0.25 correlation vs corn's stronger link)
5. US export demand vs Black Sea competition

## Key correlations
- ZC Corn: 0.50-0.65 (weather/acreage, breaks on geopolitical)
- ZS Soybeans: 0.40-0.55 (similar drivers, different demand)
- Black Sea: 0.75-0.95 (tethered to global)
- USD: -0.35 to -0.50 (stronger dollar hurts exports)
- Real rates: -0.25 to -0.40 (pro-cyclical)

## Seasonality & Events
- Oct-Nov: Planting, frost/drought affect germination
- Dec-Feb: Dormancy, winter kill risk
- Mar-May: Spring growth, late May freeze catastrophic
- Jun-Jul: Harvest selling, new-crop bearish
- Aug-May: Storage carry, contango

Events: USDA Winter Seeding (early Dec), WASDE (monthly), Crop Progress (weekly Thu), Black Sea weather (1-2hr moves via Globex), Export inspections (weekly Fri)

## Top 5 setups (with hit rates)

1. Spring freeze (May): Frost forecast drives 10-20 cent rally. Long dips if above 20MA, frost prob >40%. Stop 6-10 cents. 55-65% hit rate.

2. Post-WASDE shock: WASDE cuts stocks/yield triggers 5-15 cent rally. Long if holds prior close hr2, short if fades. 48-55% hit.

3. Black Sea weather: Russia/Ukraine frost/drought forecasts drive 10-30 cent rally over weeks. Long dips on news, stop 10-15c. 52-60% hit.

4. Harvest carry (Jun): Jul/Dec normal contango 25-35c. Short Jul long Dec, tighten into expiration. 60-65% hit.

5. Export demand: Sustained >5% WoW export growth for 2+ weeks. Long above 20MA, hold while demand accelerates. 50-58% hit.

## Traps
- Freeze hype reversal: Priced in April; mild freeze causes reversion
- Black Sea fatigue: War premium numbs traders; real tightness ignored
- Harvest yield missprediction: Short harvest; May freeze stops shorts
- Export one-week wonder: Single week strength misread as trend
- WASDE pre-trading: Rumors rally; release in-line causes fade
- Spread inversion: Contango flips to backwardation on supply shock
- Thin liquidity: 10-contract moves market 5-10 cents; use 5 max
