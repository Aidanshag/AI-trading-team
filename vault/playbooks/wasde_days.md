# USDA report days

## Trigger

- WASDE: monthly, typically 12:00 PM ET on scheduled day.
- NASS Crop Progress: Mondays 4:00 PM ET during growing season.
- Prospective Plantings: late March.
- Grain Stocks: quarterly.

## What agents must NOT do

- No new entries in the 15 min before a WASDE release or 30 min after.
- Never trade a weather thesis alone — weather is noisy and late. Combine with stocks/usage and trend data.

## Preferred structures

- Long-dated option spreads for weather-season positioning (May–Aug) in [[ZC]], [[ZS]], [[ZW]].
- Crush spreads ([[ZS]] vs [[ZL]]+[[ZM]]) — position-able as a single defined trade.

## What to watch

- Ending stocks revisions — primary driver.
- Export demand and export pace.
- Brazil/Argentina weather and currency during Southern Hemisphere growing season.
- Chinese import trajectory for soybeans.

## Exit

- Same session unless the thesis is explicit multi-week weather/supply play.
