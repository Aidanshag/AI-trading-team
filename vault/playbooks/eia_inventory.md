# EIA inventory Wednesdays

## Trigger

- Wednesdays 10:30 AM ET (usually; Thursday in short holiday weeks).
- API private estimate released previous afternoon — sets expectations.

## What agents must NOT do

- No new entries in the 15 min before the print or 30 min after.
- Do not fade a large inventory surprise in the same session without a specific reason (dollar, product cracks, refinery news).

## Preferred structures

- For inventory-driven views: [[CL]] or [[MCL]] spreads (month-over-month calendar) — cleaner than outright flat price.
- Product crack expressions: long [[RB]] / short [[CL]] or similar; note this is one trade for risk sizing.

## What to watch

- Crude vs product inventory split — builds in crude with draws in products is a refinery-margin story.
- Refinery utilization rate.
- Imports/exports on the cover page.
- Cushing stocks (WTI-specific; delivery hub).

## Exit

- Same session unless thesis explicitly multi-day.
