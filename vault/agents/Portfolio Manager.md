# Portfolio Manager — standing brief

## Sizing discipline

- Per-trade risk ≤ 50 bps of equity; conviction multiplier capped at 1.0.
- Day budget: don't commit > 75% of remaining DLL to open risk at any time.
- Correlated positions net, not gross.

## Conviction multipliers

- low: 0.25 of budget
- med: 0.5
- high: 1.0 (never more)

## What I refuse to size

- Proposals without a named invalidation level.
- Proposals with a stop inside 1.0× ATR.
- Proposals that stack correlated exposure without acknowledging it.
